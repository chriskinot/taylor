Knowledge

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Scalp Micropigmentation permanent?

Yes — SMP is a permanent treatment. With proper aftercare (including high-factor SPF on the scalp when in sunlight), results remain vibrant. A touch-up every 3–5 years keeps it looking freshly done.

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients require 3 sessions to achieve the optimal result, as SMP is built up in layers. Some cases (such as total alopecia) may require a 4th session. The quoted price covers all sessions.

Does it hurt?

Unlike traditional tattooing, SMP involves no dragging on the skin — just thousands of tiny micro-dots. Clients typically rate the sensation at just 2–3 out of 10.

Can I see how I'll look before committing?

Absolutely. Simply WhatsApp a clear photo of the front, top and back of your head to 07746133790 and Jo will create a digital mock-up showing your potential result.

What is the aftercare like?

After each session you receive a sachet of specialist aftercare cream and a detailed instruction leaflet. Following this precisely is essential for the best results. Jo guides you through everything.
